Farm Table

May 16, 2014

While outfitting the Change Collective office with furniture, we (the team) found ourselves just about out of budget, but we were still without a lunch table. We all liked the idea of a long farm table, at which we could all eat lunch together, but most tables of this type were well above what was left in our budget.

I took it as an opportunity for a fun little project. After a quick trip to Home Depot to pick up some basic lumber, I headed over to Artisan's Asylum in Somerville to begin construction. Shoutout to Derek Haswell for his assistance during the final assembly steps.
